Country challenges

1. Economic Inequality :

Thailand has one of the highest levels of economic inequality in the world, with the wealthiest 10% of the population controlling more than half of the country's wealth.

2. Climate Change :

Thailand is particularly vulnerable to the effects of climate change, including rising sea levels, extreme weather events, and water shortages.

3. Education :

Thailand's education system is in need of reform, with a lack of resources and quality teachers leading to poor educational outcomes.

4. Corruption :

Corruption is a major problem in Thailand, with the government and business sector both affected.

5. Health Care :

Thailand's health care system is in need of reform, with inadequate access to health care services and a lack of resources for those in need.
