What is broken ladder

Depending on household income and wealth, households can be divided into five (5) different economic strata, namely Bottom 10% (the lowest economic stratum), Barely managed, Middle Income, Rich and Super Rich (the highest economic stratum). The table shows the income multiple required to move up from 'Bottom 10%* (the lowest economic stratum) to various other strata of society. For example, if the value of column Middle Income is 6, it means that households from the 'Bottom 10%' economic stratum need a six fold jump in income to get into the 'Middle Income' economic stratum.

A Lorenz curve, developed by American economist Max Lorenz in 1905, is a graphical representation of income inequality or wealth inequality.
