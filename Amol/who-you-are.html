Nicaragua has all kinds of imaginable beaches, from dark sand to white sand to virgin and wild beaches with an abundance of wildlife refuges. The population is multicultural. It is popular as a tourists destination due to its biological diversity, warm tropical climate, and active volcanoes. Education is free for all Nicaraguans where elementary education is free and compulsory, but the children in the rural areas stay away from such opportunities. According to the literacy campaign, there was a remarkable decrease in the illiteracy rate. Poverty and corruption are major problems. The Nicaraguan government guarantees universal free health care for its citizens. Still, a considerable number of women have been experiencing domestic violence.
